Decision Maker Reading

User this spread to look at the situations from all perspectives and to make best possible decision. It fits "Should I" or "Should I Not" questions as well.

You: This card represents you and your energy at the time of the reading.

The Unknown: This card represents people, places, or events in your life that you're either not aware of right now, or unwilling to face.

The Known: This card represents the details you are aware of. It is also a clue as to what you can reasonably deal with right now.

The Action: This card encourages you to take a certain type of action, representative by the card's meaning.
